What is Sound Factory Music School?

In the educational setting we run provisions that teach students how to become young artists and not just musicians. There are two options for students to choose from: Rock/Pop Band sessions or Group Singing sessions.

Parents pay for these sessions however, we run a scheme that works with schools willing to use some of their Pupil Premium budget to provide either Singing Group sessions or Rock/Pop Band sessions for students that professionals within school feel would benefit from. We take student referrals and look at which groups those students would fit into to ensure a bespoke approach to each individual is taken. This takes into consideration any needs that the student may have to ensure we meet them therefore providing the best experience we can for that student.

What is included in the sessions?

In the Rock/Pop Band sessions; Students learn how to play cover songs by famous bands and artists as well as write their own music. Here they are taught how to play as part of a band where they are taught chords, basslines and beats, and create their own songs which later gets played live to parents in school. During their process they also get the opportunity to record at our studio free of any extra cost! In the Singing Group sessions; Students learn how to use their voice through guided vocal warm ups, vocal games, and through song. This is a fun way of working as part of a group to learn new skills and songs that students are familiar or not so familiar with. We occasionally partner up with some of the rock band groups in order to perform something special for parents as part of our regular performances. We also join the rock Bands in performing at a yearly showcase at the Barbican Theatre, giving students something special to remember.
