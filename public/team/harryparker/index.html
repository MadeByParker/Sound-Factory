Harry Parker

Harry Parker

Head of Website Design and Marketing Executive

Graduating with a degree in Computer Science, specialising in Cyber Security, Harry has always been driven by his desire to make a meaningful impact on people's lives through the digital realm. As technology becomes increasingly pervasive, he recognised the immense potential it holds for improving the way we connect and engage with the world.

Throughout his academic journey, Harry's passion for creating visually stunning and highly functional websites has grown. He honed his exceptional talents and meticulous attention to detail, enabling him to design captivating online experiences for a wide array of small businesses and individuals. Notable among his portfolio are successful projects like The South Hams Society and Eyes of Caelum, where Harry's innovative approach and commitment to excellence shone through.

From an early age, Harry's fascination with computers has created a deep-seated ambition to harness his IT skills for the betterment of society. His dedication to improving people's lives through technology is evident in his four years of experience in web design, both within and beyond the university setting. This rich background has provided him with a wealth of knowledge and expertise in crafting tailor-made websites that precisely meet the unique needs and aspirations of his clients.
